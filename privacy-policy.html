User Roles:

  • Customers: Browse, watch livestreams, purchase products

  • Verified Sellers: Sellers who have completed identity/business verification

Social Features:

  • Follow/unfollow sellers
  • Like and comment on livestreams and products
  • Private messaging between buyers and sellers
  • Activity feed (optional, can be made private)

Public Profiles:

  • Username, profile picture, bio are publicly visible by default

  • Livestreams and product listings are always public when active
