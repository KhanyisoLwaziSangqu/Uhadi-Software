Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the CoworkAI mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for iOS and Android devices, created by Uhadi Technology Pty (Ltd) (hereby referred to as "Service Provider", "we", "us", or "our"). This service is offered as a Freemium application with optional paid subscription tiers.

1. Information We Collect

CoworkAI collects and processes the following types of information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and display name when you create an account
  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google
  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create using the App for transcription purposes
  • Payment Information: Payment and subscription data processed through RevenueCat and Apple App Store/Google Play Store (we do not store your credit card details)
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team at uhadi@uhadi.co.za

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Recording duration, transcription language selected, features used, subscription tier, monthly usage statistics
  • App Performance: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics
  • IP Address: Used for security and fraud prevention
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data for free tier users (collected by Google AdMob)

1.3 Location Data

The Application does NOT collect precise location data from your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Process audio recordings, generate transcriptions, create AI summaries and insights
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions, track usage limits
  • AI Processing: Send audio recordings to third-party AI providers (AssemblyAI, Google Cloud Speech-to-Text, OpenAI) for transcription and analysis
  • Cloud Backup: Store transcriptions and user data securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Subscription Management: Process payments, manage billing, handle upgrades/downgrades via RevenueCat
  • Advertising: Display ads to free tier users via Google AdMob
  • Analytics: Improve app performance, understand user behavior, identify bugs
  • Communication: Send important notifications about your account, service updates, and promotional offers (you can opt out)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

CoworkAI integrates with the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services - App distribution, authentication
  • Supabase - Authentication, database storage, cloud backup
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management and billing
  • Google AdMob - Advertising for free tier users
  • AssemblyAI - AI transcription services (English, Afrikaans, and 6 other languages)
  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text - AI transcription for African languages (isiZulu, isiXhosa, Sepedi, Swahili, Yoruba, Hausa, Amharic, Shona, Igbo, Oromo, Wolof)
  • OpenAI - AI-powered summary and insights generation
  • Expo - Development framework and services

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Audio recordings are sent to AssemblyAI, Google Cloud Speech-to-Text, and OpenAI for transcription and analysis. These providers process audio according to their privacy policies and do not use your data to train their models without your consent.
  • Cloud Storage: Your account data, transcriptions, and usage statistics are stored in Supabase (AWS-hosted PostgreSQL database with encryption)
  • Payment Processing: Subscription information is shared with RevenueCat, Apple App Store, and Google Play Store
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred (you will be notified)
  • Protecting Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Audio Recordings: Stored locally on your device and optionally in cloud storage until you delete them
  • Transcriptions: Retained until you delete them from the app
  • Usage Data: Retained for 24 months for analytics purposes
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting uhadi@uhadi.co.za or deleting your account in the app.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Export your transcriptions and recordings
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (account-related emails cannot be disabled)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Advertising: Reset your advertising ID in device settings to limit ad tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at uhadi@uhadi.co.za.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • Data transmitted between the app and servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Audio files stored in Google Cloud Storage are encrypted at rest
  • Supabase database uses row-level security policies
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely and never exposed in the app
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

CoworkAI is NOT intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it from our servers.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at uhadi@uhadi.co.za.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of South Africa, including:

These transfers are necessary to provide our services. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place through:

10. Advertising (Free Tier Only)

Free tier users will see advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)
  • Device and app usage information

Premium users (Starter and Pro tiers) do not see ads.

To opt out of personalized ads:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
