About Us

Uhadi Technology is a software product company focused on building innovative AI-powered solutions. We develop two core products: CoworkAI, an AI productivity tool that transforms how teams collaborate and work, and SeeShop, a video and livestream social shopping platform that revolutionizes online retail.

Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology to create products that make a real difference in how people work and shop. We also offer custom software development services for businesses looking to build their own innovative solutions.

Khanyiso Lwazi Sangqu, Founder & CEO

Khanyiso is a self-taught software developer with a passion for building software - web development in particular. He has a prior background in corporate communications. Khanyiso graduated from the University of Cape Town with a Bachelor of Social Sciences.
